Frequently Asked Questions

Timings

Are the classes only one hour long?

Ahmed's Institute takes complete responsibility for the child’s After College/School. In our experience we have found that the following classes need to study for the following amount of time:

  • Engineering Class: 4 hrs daily
  • Diploma Class: 3 hrs daily needed
  • School/Intermediate Class: 3 hrs daily needed
    • Ahmed's Institute takes the responsibility for all of these hours. We divide the time in the following way:
  • Live Interaction session – 1 hour daily which is fixed
  • Worksheets, Assignments etc given on Google Classroom based on areas where the child needs practice. The amount of work given is based on the gaps in your child’s learning.

How many days a week will your tuition be conducted?

6 days a week from Monday to Saturday. Special Classes On Sunday.

How much time will you give to my child in that 3 hours?

The child will be utilizing that whole hours. The way our system works, the attention of the tutor will be available to the child whenever he needs it.

Do you have Morning tuitions?

Yes,We have morning tuitions, Only for cets Exams.

How do you complete the portion of exam before the exam?

It is possible to complete the portion before the exams as we follow the college/School syllabus and go at the same pace as the college/School.

About Demo Classes ?

Do you offer Demo Classes?

Demo classes in tuition actually do not serve any purpose – takes ⅔ days for your child and tutor to understand each other and to get comfortable with each other. You will not get any idea of this from a 1-hour demo class.That's why we offer 2 days Demo class.

Is it a one to one class?

We believe children learn better when they are allowed to learn together, this is why we do not have one to one class. These classes also promote friendly competition and allow children to have more fun while studying.
However, we have a customizes plan which makes sure that each child gets individual attention as and when we need it

Is it group tuition?

We have classes with a maximum of 15 to 20 students of the same class and curriculum at one time.

Will you conduct any exams or tests and when?

We conduct many different tests to see whether the child has grasped the topics taught in class. These tests include Chapter Wise Tests, daily worksheets and assignments. They are conducted regularly. We also conduct Concept Evaluation Tests to see the child’s conceptual clarity.
